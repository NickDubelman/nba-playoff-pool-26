## Key Narratives

### 🔴 Major Injury Concerns

- **Luka Dončić (LAL)** — Grade 2 left hamstring strain. Out for all of R1 (starts April 18). Traveling to Spain for specialized treatment. Return timeline 3–6 weeks. At best, returns for R2 if Lakers survive. **Do not draft unless you're willing to gamble.**

- **Austin Reaves (LAL)** — Also injured alongside Luka, out for the same period. Lakers are badly undermanned heading into the bracket.

- **Victor Wembanyama (SAS)** — Left rib contusion (collision April 6). No fracture. Reported likely to return this week before the season ends. **Monitor closely — expected to be ready for playoffs.** Will likely wear a flak jacket.

- **Jaylen Brown (BOS)** — Left Achilles tendinitis. Being managed carefully down the stretch. Brown says it's "just soreness" and expects to be fine. Celtics are being cautious. **Low-medium concern.**

- **Cade Cunningham (DET)** — Missed since March 17 with a collapsed lung. Returned April 8 (13 pts, 10 ast vs Milwaukee). Rust expected, but timing is perfect. **Monitor his conditioning.**

- **Jayson Tatum (BOS)** — Ruptured Achilles in June 2025 (NBA Finals). Only played 16 games this season. Coming back to form slowly. PPG (21.8) over small sample. **Significant injury risk; small sample size.**

- **Jalen Williams (OKC)** — Missed much of the season with a hamstring + wrist. Returned March 23. Reportedly feeling great and ramping up for playoffs. **Key upside play if fully healthy on a 20-game team.**

### 🟢 Positive Storylines

- **Detroit Pistons are the real deal.** At 57-22, this is no fluke. Jalen Duren is having an All-NBA caliber season (22.7 PPG, 10.1 RPG, 69.8% FG in recent stretch). This is Detroit's first deep playoff run in two decades.

- **OKC is even scarier with Jalen Williams back.** SGA at 31.1 PPG is the MVP frontrunner. Add a returning Jalen Williams (17.1 PPG regular season) and Chet Holmgren (17.1 PPG, elite rim protection) — they are legitimately terrifying.

- **San Antonio's rise is historic.** Wembanyama averaging 24.8 PPG / 11.5 RPG / 3.1 BLK in year 3. The Spurs went 61-19 — their best record since the Duncan dynasty. De'Aaron Fox (traded midseason from SAC) added 18.5 PPG. Stephon Castle (16.8 PPG) is a rising star.

- **Kevin Durant / Houston pairing exceeded expectations.** KD at 25.9 PPG in 77 games. The trade (Jalen Green + picks → Phoenix) is being called a massive steal. Alperen Sengun (20.3 PPG) gives them a lethal second option.

### ⚠️ Draft Strategy Notes

- **Play-in teams are very risky.** Booker, Kawhi, Maxey, Curry — all high PPG scorers but their teams need to win 1–2 play-in games just to get in. If they lose the 7v8 game, they're done in 1 game. Avoid unless you're getting them at deep value.
- **Lakers are a wildcard.** Without Luka for R1, they could lose to Houston. Even if Luka returns for R2, he's coming off a Grade 2 hamstring — post-injury explosiveness is unpredictable.
- **Denver + Murray is often undervalued.** Murray notoriously elevates in the playoffs (2020, 2023 champions). The 3-seed in the West could go deep.
