Research the team named in `$ARGUMENTS` for an NBA Playoffs "Scoring Pool" where cumulative playoff points determine the winner.

1. **Playoff Seeding & Bracket** — Search for the team's current seed and first-round matchup. Note whether they're in the East or West.

2. **Projected Path** — Estimate round-by-round how far this team is likely to advance, and the expected number of total games played (best case / likely case / early exit). Teams going deep = more games = more points.

3. **Top Scorers & Usage** — Search for the team's scoring hierarchy heading into the playoffs:
   - Primary scorer (highest PPG, likely usage)
   - Secondary scorers worth considering
   - Any depth chart changes due to injuries or lineup shifts

4. **Team Narratives** — Any notable storylines: coaching changes, recent form, tough/easy bracket path, rivalry matchups, home court advantage, fatigue concerns.

5. **Draft Recommendations** — For each notable player on this team, give a quick rating:
   - Name | PPG | Injury risk | Draft priority (High / Medium / Low / Skip)

Keep the response concise and scannable — the user may be in an active draft.
